Wedding Journal

Thursday 25th Let the party begin!

21.00 – 02.00 - Welcome drinks at beach Bar Bocampo! (All guests are welcome)
Dress code: Flip flops, swimwear & light summer clothes.
Tip: Bring along towels as night swimming might occur. 😉

You can also discover the little treasures that the island of Aegina has to offer, including the ancient temple of Afea, the Monastery of Saint Nectarios and Moni Island. For more information on the island you can consult the ‘We Love Aegina’ website.

Friday 26th Enjoy the sun!

13.00 - 18.00 - Pool party at Hotel Danae! (All guests are welcome)
Dress code: Flip flops, swimwear & light summer clothes.
Note: Finger food and refreshments will be provided.

21.30-12.00 - Dinner at restaurant Axivadaki! (Families only)
Dress code: Breezy and summery.

Saturday 27th Wedding day!

16.00 - Andreas’s wedding preparations at Hotel Danae with traditions, drinking and dancing!

17.00 - Nathalie’s wedding preparations at Villa Saliarelis with traditions, drinking and dancing!

18.15 - Andreas and team Groom departs for church.

18.50 - Nathalie and team Bride departs for church.

19.00 - Wedding time at Chapel of ‘Agioi Apostoloi’.

20.00 - Photo shooting newlyweds with friends and family around the chapel! Stick around as we want a shot with you!

21.00 - Wedding reception – sited dinner at Villa Saliarelis (located opposite the chapel) and dancing till first light of dawn!

Dress code: Cocktail and Black-tie.
